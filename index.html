Recruitment

I plan to recruit graduate students on these projects:

  • stellar or planetary dynamo;

  • tidal wave and turbulence in star or planet;

  • star-planet or planet-moon tidal and magnetic interactions;

  • radio emissions from star-planet magnetic interaction;

  • circumbinary disk dynamics;

  • protoplanetary disk with self-gravity, cooling and magnetic field;

  • quasi-periodic oscillations in black-hole disk.

Students with the background of astronomy or any related subject are welcome to contact me. The projects are not limited to the above ones and you are encouraged to propose your own ideas.
