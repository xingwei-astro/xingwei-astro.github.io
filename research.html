Research interests

My research interests are physical processes of planets, stars and disks. For example, how planet forms and evolves, how planetary orbit evolves, how planetary and stellar magnetic fields are generated and evolve, how tidal waves propagate and dissipate, and why protoplanetary or black-hole disk becomes unstable, etc. To understand these phenomena, knowledges of orbital dynamics, thermodynamics, fluid dynamics and electromagnetism are needed. Mathematical models are built and studied with order-of-magnitude estimations, paper-and-pencil calculations, and a little numerical computations. Below are some objects that I study.
